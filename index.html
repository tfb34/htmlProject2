The game starts out with Leon in search for the President's daughter in some backdrop hillbilly rural Spanish village. She is taken hostage by this crazy cult and the mission is to save her ass. So Leon must now venture on foot in search of this cult that is harbouring something quite insidious. BLAH BLAH
